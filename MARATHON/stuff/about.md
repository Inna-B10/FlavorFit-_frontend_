Welcome to FlavorFit, your all-in-one platform for personalized nutrition, fitness, and meal planning. Designed to inspire healthier lifestyles, FlavorFit empowers users to take control of their wellness journey through thoughtful design, intuitive features, and actionable insights.

Let’s explore how each module works together to make healthy living both achievable and enjoyable.

### Dashboard

This screen introduces FlavorFit’s main dashboard, a personalized hub designed to help users manage their daily nutrition and activities.

It displays an overview of the day’s schedule, highlighting key activities like meals, workouts, and hydration reminders. Users can track essential metrics such as water intake, calorie consumption, and physical activity progress, all visualized in an easy-to-understand format.

A shopping list feature allows users to manage groceries efficiently, with AI-powered suggestions enhancing convenience. A curated recipe suggestion panel ties into user preferences, offering actionable inspiration for healthy meals.

### Recipe Details

The Recipe Details screen immerses users in a step-by-step cooking experience. Each recipe comes with a detailed breakdown of ingredients, preparation instructions, and nutritional information.

Vibrant imagery guides users through each stage, ensuring clarity and ease. A community-driven comment section allows users to share feedback, tips, or modifications, fostering engagement and collaboration.

### Order

This screen simplifies grocery delivery management. Users can view current orders, track delivery progress in real time with courier updates, and check estimated arrival times. A detailed list of items ensures order accuracy, and the friendly design reinforces trust and transparency throughout the process.

### Recipe Discovery

The Recipe Discovery screen empowers users to explore a wide variety of dishes tailored to their preferences. With filters for dietary goals, meal types, and cuisines, users can quickly find recipes that align with their nutritional needs. Popular and recommended recipes are prominently featured, with clear indicators for difficulty level, calorie count, and preparation time. This feature transforms meal planning into an engaging and enjoyable experience.

### Personal info

This screen allows users to manage their personal information and set tailored fitness and nutrition goals. Users can input and track details like body measurements, activity levels, and desired outcomes (e.g., weight loss). A visual representation of progress creates a motivating and user-friendly experience, ensuring that FlavorFit adapts to individual goals seamlessly.

Thank you for exploring FlavorFit, a platform built to make healthy living easier, more enjoyable, and tailored to your unique journey. We’re excited to help you achieve your wellness goals with innovative features and a supportive design that works for you. Here’s to making better choices, one step at a time!
